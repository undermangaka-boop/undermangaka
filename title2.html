UNDER-MANGA

Five years of silence. Zero plot progress. One very grumpy fox tail. This is the story of a manga abandoned in 2020 that suddenly claws its way back to life in 2025.

The author swears there’s a plan, but let’s be real: the "plan" is just beautifully rendered chaos and "pre-release promo art." Welcome behind the scenes of the slowest creative process in history!
